Alice VR - Project Wonderland

Engine: Unity 5.5 | Duration: 1 semester | Role: Programmer

Platform: Oculus Rift

Wonderland is a project with the Alice Team. Out goal is to explore the potential for virtual reality to make abstract computer science concepts understandable for grade school students. We are focused on creating VR experimences to teach different CS ideas, playing tests with faculties from local education zone, and reasearching the effects of VR. You can check details from our website.

We overall researched tones of existing games/experiences and created 6 main prototypes:
   ✦ Cute Fractal - concept of recursion, function chaining
   ✦ Solar System - concept of object-oriented programming
   ✦ Color Cube - concept of binary search
   ✦ Flying Flags - concept of linked list
   ✦ Buggy Factory - concept of unit Testing
   ✦ Jumping Robot - concept of parameter passing of functions

Except coding, I also tried as a designer in this project. I designed Cute Fractal and Color Cube.
