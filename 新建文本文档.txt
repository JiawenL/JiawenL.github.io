		<br>
		My role is algorithm implement and testing.<br>
		<br>
		We started from traditional narrowband linear prediction based method, which at last had a bit rate of 26kb/s and a SNR of -0.012dB. Then we went to source-filter model. And finally we used CELP mechanism and achieve one with 10kb/s bit rate(if use binary data instead of char it could reduced to 5kb/s) and 2.99dB SNR.<br>
