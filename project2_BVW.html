Build Virtual World

Build Virtual World is a project focus course in ETC. We build five games with different platforms - kinect, Oculus Rift, Leap Motion, Eye Gazer, Ps move in group of five(two programmers, two artists, one sound designer).

My Role for BVW is programmer. Finally two of my five games made into the festival(17 out of 75). All games in BVW are made with Unity.


Harsh Light

Harsh Light is the game we made with Eye Gazer in one week.

In the game player can guide a fairy with a target controled by your eyes. Player should help fairy avoid all the traps, eascape from the dark cave, and try to win a high score.

For this game, I implemented controler for all traps, traps generate interfaces, score system and score bored.


Muse Meow

Muse Meow is a game we made with PS Move in three weeks.

In this game a happy cat jumps out of a painting - The Scream cause a dog is chasing him. Player should help the cat with PS Move to go through HomoVitruvianus, The Persistence of Memory, Mona Lisa, The Shrimps, Last Supper, escape from the dog, and go back to his original painting!

For this game, I implemented the event controler for the whole story and auto-checkpoints for each step. To make more fun, we sing together to make alive background music for it.


I also made a Kinect game ask player to kill cockroach and protect girlfriend, a Oculus experience game let player travel around a virtual world and solve fractional puzzles, a hologram game that player can summon Santa!
