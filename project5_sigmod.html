SIGMOD 2014 Programming Contest

ACM SIGMOD is the ACM Special Interest Group on Management of Data. It sponsors the annual SIGMOD/PODS conference, one of the most important and selective in the field[1]. It also holds a programming contest each year, and will invite the first five teams to present their algorithm during the conference.

Our team 'blxlrsmb' won the 4th in the 2014 contest[2]. Here is our poster .

I mainly designed and implemented the algorithm for Query 2. And we discussed Query 4, parallel, and some other details together.
